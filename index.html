Quick Start Guide

1

Create MetaMask wallet

Create a MetaMask Wallet using either a desktop computer or an iOS/Android mobile device. That will allow you to buy, sell, send, and receive Fifa Official Token.

2

Send BNB or BUSD Bep20 to your wallet

You can buy BNB or BUSD transfer it to your MetaMask Wallet from exchanges like Coinbase, Binance. Make sure to use the Smart Chain network when transfering BNB.

3

Connect your wallet to PancakeSwap

Access your wallet to Pancakeswap by clicking Connect to a wallet and selecting MetaMask.

4

Swap BNB or BUSD for Fifa Official Token

You can start swapping as soon as you have Fifa Official Token. available! Press Select a token and enter the token address or search for it on the tokens list.
